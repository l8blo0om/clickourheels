The Story Behind the Booth

Created by a Dancer,
For This Community

Click Our Heels was built by dancers who got tired of watching incredible moments disappear. The costume. The pose. The look in someone's eyes right after a perfect turn. These things deserve to be captured — not by an overpriced event photographer with a two-week delay and a watermarked proof sheet.

Charlotte's dance scene runs deep — salsa socials, ballroom competitions, recitals, galas, Latin weekenders. It's a community that lives on the floor and deserves a photo experience that actually matches the energy in the room.

So we built one. A full-length mirror booth that moves with the community — showing up at events, partnering with studios, and making sure every dancer walks away with something they're proud to share.

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for recital season (typically April–June), which is our busiest time. For off-season events, 2–3 weeks is usually plenty — but the sooner, the better to secure your date.
We arrive 60–90 minutes before your event start time to set up and test everything. Breakdown typically takes 30–45 minutes after the rental period ends. All of this is outside your contracted rental hours — your time is your time.

Our mirror booth footprint is surprisingly compact — we need roughly a 8×8 ft area including the backdrop. We've worked in theater lobbies, dance studio waiting rooms, school gymnasiums, and banquet halls. Just let us know the space and we'll make it work.
We serve the greater Charlotte area including Ballantyne, Huntersville, Concord, Gastonia, Monroe, and surrounding communities. Travel outside our standard radius may include a small travel fee — just reach out and we'll work it out.
